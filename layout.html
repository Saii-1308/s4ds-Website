ABOUT US

Our History

The S4DS (Society for Data Science) chapter at Dr. D. Y. Patil Institute of Technology, Pimpri was founded on 23rd September 2022 and officially inaugurated on 1st November 2023. With 96 student members and 6 faculty coordinators, the chapter aims to foster learning, innovation, and collaboration in data science and AI.


Guided by national mentors — Mr. Amol Goje, Dr. Amlan Chakraborti, and Dr. Neha Sharma — S4DS-DIT has evolved into a dynamic student-led body. The chapter focuses on bridging the gap between theory and practice while promoting technical excellence, interdisciplinary thinking, and professional development to build future-ready, data-driven minds..

Vision & Mission

Vision: To create a world where data literacy is accessible to all, and data-driven solutions transform every sector of society.

Mission: We empower students from all disciplines to harness the power of data through education, practical experience, and community building.

Core Values:

  • Inclusive learning environment
  • Practical, hands-on education
  • Collaboration across disciplines
  • Ethical data practices
  • Community impact through data
